Campus Life

School Library

PICT Model School has a well equipped library to inculcate reading habits in students from the early childhood. The books open many horizons of thoughts for the little creative minds and allows them to explore a lot of new things.

Math and ICT Lab

PICT Model school has a Math and ICT lab where students learn and understand the day to day progress in computers and technology. The use of the machines have entered in our everyday lives and it has become important to know and learn how the technology works.

Physical education

Exercise, Yoga, sports and other physical activities play an important role in keeping the students active and energetic. We have dedicated sessions for sports, yoga and physical education. Learners at PICT are trained to play sports like badminton, basketball and table tennis besides local and indigenous games to develop their motor skills and team spirit.

kitchen garden

PICT Model School has adapted this very important need of today’s lives which is to grow organic vegetables and fruits in one’s own garden! We encourage our students to learn and follow the same so as to make them understand the need and importance of organic farming.
